# Responsible Disclosure Policy

All research in this repository was conducted through authorized bug bounty platforms. No unauthorized access was performed at any point.

## Process

A finding is documented here only after one of the following conditions is met:
- The vendor has confirmed resolution
- The platform has triaged the report as a duplicate
- Explicit permission has been granted for public disclosure

The proof-of-concept included with each report uses the minimum steps needed to demonstrate impact. No data was exfiltrated, no users were affected, and no systems were damaged during testing.

## On Duplicates

A duplicate finding means another researcher submitted the same issue first. It does not mean the vulnerability is invalid. Duplicates are documented here because they represent real security research — the gap was identified independently, the PoC was built, and the report was written to standard.
